Hosterino

Shows you when you are hosting someone, and reminds you when you've forgotten to do so.

Hosterino checks the status of hosting once per minute.

The extension icon will glow purple if you are currently hosting someone. You will see a notification on all Twitch webpages about your current hosting status, and changes in it.
